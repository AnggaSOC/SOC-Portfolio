# SOC-Portfolio

## 👤 About Me
Hello! I am a **Junior Cybersecurity** focused on building a career as a **Junior SOC Analyst**. 

What sets my approach apart is my foundation in **Red Teaming (Penetration Testing)**. I believe that strong *defense* stems from a deep understanding of how *offense* works. By understanding the tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used by attackers, I can analyze logs, detect anomalies, and build defense systems and incident *playbooks* on the **Blue Team** side much more effectively and accurately.

This portfolio documents the *hands-on labs* and cybersecurity projects I have worked on.
